Neartown Ghost NFTs

Neartownghost is a play to earn game built on the near Blockchain. A game where players are invited to fight, survive and protect a town (Neartown), from the Invaders (Ghost), in order to defeat the invader you have to select one among the chosen ones (characters) to take on the form of your own ghost (Special costume). Apart from using the character to battle, the characters can also be traded. The characters have been designed into Non fungible tokens (NFTs).
